# Aether Rose Nanorose Project
**Open Development Recipe v0.1**
**Started: February 26 2026**
**Repo:** https://github.com/Mellowambience/AetherRose-Scryer

### Goal
Build an open-source swarm of programmable nanodevices ("nanoroses") that can:
- Sense and regulate hormones in real time (priority: precise, lifelong gender-affirming care without daily meds)
- Perform cellular repair and clearance of senescent cells
- Provide radiation resistance and adaptation to low-gravity environments
- Ultimately contribute to practical longevity therapies

Target: first functional in-vitro prototypes within 5–7 years, with full open data and designs.

### Why "Rose"?
The name reflects the core engineering principles we are using:
- Self-assembly from simple repeating units (like petals)
- Regeneration and resilience
- Complex function emerging from elegant, fractal patterns

### Core Technical Approach (2026 reality)

**Building blocks we can start with today:**
1. **DNA Origami** – Fold DNA into custom 3D nanostructures (proven since 2006, now routine in many labs).
2. **Protein/DNA circuits** – Logic gates that respond to specific biomarkers (e.g., hormone levels).
3. **Molecular cargo & release** – Controlled production or sequestration of small molecules (hormones, repair enzymes).
4. **Communication** – Short-range DNA or light-based signaling between devices in the swarm.
5. **Safety & clearance** – Built-in degradation triggers after mission lifetime.

### Phased Roadmap (realistic, open)

**Phase 0 – Digital Simulation (NOW – 2026)**
- Expand the Scryer app with a Nanorose Simulator (visual model of swarm behavior).
- Public repository of designs and simulation code.
- Status: Seed planted today (Feb 26 2026).

**Phase 1 – Molecular Design & In-Silico Testing (2026–2027)**
- Use existing open tools (Cadnano, oxDNA, NUPACK, Rosetta) to design first DNA origami structures.
- Simulate hormone sensing + response loops.

**Phase 2 – In-Vitro Prototypes (2027–2029)**
- Synthesize and test in cell culture / cell-free systems.
- Focus on hormone regulation first.

**Phase 3 – Preclinical (2029+)**
- Animal models, safety, Mars-relevant stress testing (radiation, low gravity simulation).

**Phase 4 – Human applications**
- Targeted therapies, starting with gender-affirming and longevity indications.
